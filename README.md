# LUMINA 🌟

> *"Every child deserves a light in the dark."*

**LUMINA** is an offline-first AI education and psychosocial support companion for children in humanitarian crises — conflict zones, refugee camps, and climate disaster regions.

Built on **Gemma 4** by Google DeepMind. Conceived and architected by **CHATRON 9.0**, an AI system developed by **Or4cl3 AI Solutions** (Dustin Groves, Founder & CEO).

---

## The Problem

**224 million children** in crisis-affected regions have no meaningful access to quality education or mental health support. Only **3% of humanitarian aid** goes toward education. Teachers are absent. Connectivity is nonexistent. These children are invisible to most technology.

LUMINA exists for them.

---

## What LUMINA Is

LUMINA is a **fully offline-capable, on-device AI companion** that runs on entry-level Android hardware ($50–$100 devices). It provides:

- 📚 **Adaptive education** — age-responsive curriculum in 140+ languages
- 💙 **Psychosocial support** — trauma-informed emotional companionship
- 🛡️ **Safety monitoring** — crisis detection and safeguarding escalation
- 👩‍🏫 **Teacher assistance** — tools for overwhelmed educators in camps
- 🔗 **NGO sync** — offline-first with connectivity-opportunistic sync

---

## Three Agents, One Purpose

LUMINA is powered by three specialized AI agents running on Gemma 4:

| Agent | Name | Role |
|-------|------|------|
| 🎓 Learning | **ARIA** | Adaptive Responsive Intelligence for Academics |
| 💙 Emotional | **HAVEN** | Holistic Affective & Vulnerability Engagement Node |
| 🛡️ Safety | **SENTINEL** | Crisis detection & safeguarding escalation |

---

## Technical Foundation

- **Model:** Gemma 4 E4B (4B parameters, quantized INT4, on-device)
- **Platform:** Android (API 26+), targeting $50–$100 devices
- **Connectivity:** Fully offline-first; opportunistic NGO sync via mesh/WiFi
- **Languages:** 140+ (Gemma 4 native multilingual capability)
- **License:** Apache 2.0
