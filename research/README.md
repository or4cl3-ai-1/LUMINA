This directory contains the research foundation underpinning LUMINA's design decisions.

## Key Reference Areas

### Humanitarian Education
- INEE Minimum Standards for Education in Emergencies (2010, updated 2024)
- UNICEF Education in Emergencies annual reports
- HCR data on school-age children in displacement

### Trauma-Informed Care
- SAMHSA's Six Principles of Trauma-Informed Care
- Psychological First Aid (WHO/UNHCR field guide)
- Child-Friendly Spaces operational guidance (UNICEF)

### Child Safeguarding in Digital Contexts
- UNICEF's Digital Safeguarding framework
- Save the Children digital safeguarding policies
- GDPR for minors / COPPA considerations for humanitarian contexts

### AI & Education Research
- Adaptive learning systems in low-resource environments
- On-device LLM performance benchmarks (edge deployment)
- Multilingual LLM performance on low-resource languages

### Mental Health in Humanitarian Crises
- IASC Guidelines on Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (2007)
- WHO mhGAP Humanitarian Intervention Guide
- Evidence base for psychosocial interventions with displaced children
