## The Problem

- **224 million children** in humanitarian crises need education support (UNICEF, 2024)
- **Only 3%** of humanitarian aid is directed toward education
- Most existing EdTech requires reliable internet, modern hardware, and English proficiency
- Mental health support for crisis-affected children is almost entirely absent from technology solutions
- Children in camps and conflict zones are **invisible to the technology industry**

---

## The Solution: LUMINA

LUMINA is an **offline-first, on-device AI education and psychosocial support companion** for crisis-affected children aged 5–17.

### How Gemma 4 Makes LUMINA Possible

LUMINA would not be possible without Gemma 4. Specifically:

| Gemma 4 Capability | How LUMINA Uses It |
|-------------------|--------------------|
| On-device deployment (E2B/E4B) | Runs with zero internet on $50 Android devices |
| 140+ language support | Serves virtually every refugee population natively |
| Multimodal (text + image) | Visual learning activities without internet |
| 256K context window | Maintains full session history for continuity |
| Apache 2.0 license | Enables free deployment to NGOs globally |
| Multi-step agentic capability | Powers three coordinated agents (ARIA, HAVEN, SENTINEL) |

### The Three Agents

**ARIA** (Learning Agent) — Adaptive education companion. Meets children at their actual level, not their age-expected level. Never tests, never grades, always celebrates.

**HAVEN** (Emotional Support Agent) — Trauma-informed psychosocial companion. Opens every session. Signals emotional readiness to ARIA. Provides grounding, validation, and crisis-adjacent support.

**SENTINEL** (Safety Agent) — Silent background monitor. Detects safeguarding concerns. Triggers appropriate protocols. Encrypts everything. Transmits nothing without consent.
